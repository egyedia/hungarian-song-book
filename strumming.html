Strumming Patterns

For the songs in this book, these few strumming patterns cover most of the repertoire. The detailed teaching is in the book; here is the summary.

1. Simple downstrokes

↓ ↓ ↓ ↓

For the first songs and children's songs.

Book: from p. 56

2. Boom-chuck (Esztam)

↓₂ ⇓₄ ↓₂ ⇓₄

Two strings unaccented, four strings accented — Hungarian folk songs, csárdás.

Book: p. 64

3. Standard strum

↓ ↓ ↑ ↑ ↓ ↑

Pop-rock songs, campfire classics.

Book: Community singing chapter

4. Slow asymmetric

↓   ↓

Two downstrokes per bar, the second slightly "rushing": a 40–60% ratio — slow lyrical folk songs.

Book: p. 78, Szép szivárvány

5. Four-stroke with mute

↓ ↓ ✕ ↓〜 ↓

Two quick + mute + a long ringing stroke + a closing stroke — songs with a special rhythm.

Book: p. 90, Elmegyek, elmegyek
